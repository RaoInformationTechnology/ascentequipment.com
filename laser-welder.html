500W Laser Pro

The only laser welder of its kind that capable of welding aluminum!

  • The Ascent Laser Welder Pro 500W also welds Stainless Steel, Galvanized Steel, Copper, Brass and Titanium giving you un-matched flexibility and performance from one machine.
  • Laser welding is completely safe and the simple function of “fusing” the metals together provides a strong permanent weld that does not require cleaning, buffing or polishing.
  • Laser welding eliminates the need for additional materials such as welding wire etc. providing a very clean operation which is considerably better for the environment.
  • The Ascent Laser Welder 500W Pro is extremely easy to use and does not require skilled labor to operate.
  • The operator monitors the activity on CCTV and the laser position is adjustable for applications where narrow channel welding is required.
  • The Ascent Laser Welder Pro 500W is equipped with a 3 HP water cooled chiller for laser cooling which only runs on inexpensive distilled water.
  • The optional Hand-held Laser Torch provides flexibility when welding complex and oversize letters or logos. All Ascent Laser Welders are FDA approved.
  • They are designed for low maintenance and come complete with operator safety goggles, instruction manuals and safety instructions to meet all USA and Canadian published guidelines for Class IV laser equipment.
  • Installation of every machine is performed by our certified engineer who will also provide full training to your staff.
