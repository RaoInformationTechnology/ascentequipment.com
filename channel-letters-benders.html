Features

The Ascent AT3 CNC Bending Machines provide the lowest cost production of “new style” channel letters without trim and reverse/halo letters. The AT3 forms aluminum letters in thickness ranging from .026” to 0.63” (.66mm to 1.6mm) and stainless steel letters up to 1mm (22 AWG) thick and in heights from 1” to 8”. Letters can be produced using our non-proprietary Ascent extrusion/profiles that are UL certified for indoor and outdoor to quickly and easily make.
