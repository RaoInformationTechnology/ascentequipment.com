Ascent CNC Trim Machine

A MUST FOR EVERY SIGN MANUFACTURER REGARDLESS OF WHICH BENDING MACHINE YOU HAVE!

  • The NEW Ascent Trim Machine is an “industry first” that converts flat aluminum into trim and then automatically forms the trim into the shape of the letter.
  • Aluminum trim will not fade and crack over time when exposed to UV light.
  • A single operator now creates the trim and letter shapes making the Ascent Trim Machine the most versatile and economical to operate.
  • With the Ascent Trim Machine, quality and repeatability guaranteed for every letter. Acrylic faces are easily installed to the trim using adhesive that formulated to glue aluminum and acrylic.
  • There is no longer a need for pegboard or wood nails or magnetic tables and hand shaping meaning your labor costs are greatly reduced.
  • The inexpensive flat coil aluminum comes in 1”, 2” and 3” for larger letters and in an assortment of colors.
  • All machines include installation, start up and training by our engineers form our offices in Buffalo, NY.
